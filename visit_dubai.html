Burj Khalifa

At over 828 metres, Burj Khalifa is the tallest building in the world with an observation deck that offers panoramic city, desert and ocean views.

Dubai Desert Safari

For an adrenaline-pumping adventure, sign up for a desert safari and head out for dune bashing adventure in a 4x4 vehicle, filled with thrilling twists and turns.

The Dubai Fountain

The largest choreographed fountain system in the world, The Dubai Fountain on Burj Khalifa Lake shoots water up to 152 metres, and is illuminated by 6,600 lights.

ORDER

Dubai Opera House

Designed to resemble a dhow, Dubai Opera House can seat up to 2,000 patrons. It hosts a variety of events like theatrical shows, opera performances, ballet recitals, concerts and exhibitions.

ORDER

Jumeirah Mosque

Welcoming people of all faiths, Jumeirah Mosque is a prominent city landmark that flaunts classic medieval architecture, and is constructed in stone with elaborate domes and minarets.

ORDER

Palm Jumeirah

The iconic manmade island of Palm Jumeirah is famous for its trademark palm tree shape and houses waterfront bungalows, plush boutiques, luxury hotels and upscale restaurants.

Dhow Cruise Through Dubai Creek

A traditional Arab sea-trading vessel made out of wood, take a dhow cruise through Dubai Creek at night and watch the city skyline glittering with lights.

Walk at JBR

The Walk at Jumeirah Beach Residence (JBR) is a popular spot by the sea where you can shop, dine and stroll. Always breezy, this lovely boulevard will not fail to satiate your retail cravings. From fashion, home décor, handicraft shops, and boutiques to restaurants, there’s a lot to explore here.

Jumeirah Beach

Stretched along the coast of the historic Al Faihidi district, Jumeirah Beach is a sandy white strip of sand overlooking the serene waters. The cluster of high-end resorts and properties add glitz to the lively waterfront.

ORDER

Dubai Museum

Within the Al Fahidi Fort is the Dubai Museum that offers an insight into the history of the United Arab Emirates, and houses precious artefacts and antiques..

ORDER

Dubai Marina

An artificial canal city, Dubai Marina is a high-end lifestyle complex with luxury hotels, trendy restaurants and cafés, and boutique shops.

Kite Beach

Spanning two kilometres along the Arabian Gulf, Kite Beach is a public beach with lots of facilities including jogging tracks, a skate park, a beach library and food trucks.
